About Suzi's Bakeshop!

We currently have one shop located near Downtown Cincinnati. We are located at 101 E Freedom Way, right across from Great American Ballpark. Looking to expand to our 2nd location in Cleveland next year!!!

I opened shop back in 2016 and we have seen tremendous growth ever since. We hope you continue to enjoy the many treats our shop has to offer!
